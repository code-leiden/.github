# Contributing to Code Leiden

Welcome to Code Leiden! We're thrilled that you're interested in contributing to our coding club community. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, there's a place for you here.

## How to Join

We welcome everyone from Leiden and surrounding areas who is interested in coding, learning, and building together! Here's how you can get started:

1. **Introduce yourself** - Join our community channels and say hello
2. **Explore our projects** - Check out the projects section below to see what we're working on
3. **Ask questions** - Don't hesitate to ask for help or clarification
4. **Start contributing** - Pick a project that interests you and dive in!

We're particularly excited to welcome new members from Leiden. Our community is built on the principles of learning together, supporting each other, and creating awesome projects.

## Projects to Work On

We maintain a variety of projects suitable for different skill levels. Here are some areas where you can contribute:

### Beginner-Friendly Projects
- **Tutorial Projects** - Help create or improve tutorials for learning various programming languages
- **Documentation** - Improve existing documentation, fix typos, or add examples
- **Website Updates** - Contribute to our community website with content or design improvements

### Intermediate Projects
- **Web Development** - Build web applications using modern frameworks
- **API Development** - Create and improve RESTful APIs
- **Data Analysis** - Work on data visualization and analysis projects

### Advanced Projects
- **Open Source Contributions** - Contribute to major open source projects as a team
- **System Design** - Architect and build scalable systems
- **Machine Learning** - Develop ML models and AI applications

### Community Projects
- **Workshop Materials** - Create content for our regular workshops
- **Code Challenges** - Design coding challenges for members to solve
- **Mentorship Programs** - Help newer members learn and grow

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork the repository** you want to contribute to
2. **Create a new branch** for your feature or fix (`git checkout -b feature/amazing-feature`)
3. **Make your changes** and commit them with clear, descriptive messages
4. **Push to your fork** (`git push origin feature/amazing-feature`)
5. **Open a Pull Request** and describe your changes

## Code Review Process

All contributions go through a review process:
- At least one other member will review your code
- We'll provide constructive feedback
- Once approved, your contribution will be merged

## Getting Help

If you need help at any point:
- Ask in our community channels
- Reach out to project maintainers
- Attend our regular meetups and workshops

## Community Guidelines

Please read our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) to understand our community standards and expectations.

Thank you for being part of Code Leiden! Together, we'll build amazing things and grow as developers.
